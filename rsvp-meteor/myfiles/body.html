Embracing Error to Enable Rapid Crowdsourcing

Based on research published in this paper.

Our goal will be to separate a set of dog images from the images on the right. When you click play, a random set of images will be flashed in front of you. Press a spacebar whenever you see a dog.
Don't worry about reacting late, our model accounts for worker delays and will attempt to pick out the correct dog images. Everytime you do the task, the model will update its belief of what dogs look like and will move the most likely dog images to the top. The model will also print precision and recall curves at the bottom so you can track your progress.
